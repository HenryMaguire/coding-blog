Talking Machines with Henry

Hi, my name is Henry and welcome to my blog. I'm a Physics PhD student in Manchester and over the last year or so I've got really interested in AI and Machine Learning. I have a couple of ongoing projects which I would like to share with the world and I'm hoping that this blog will help to develop my ideas.

One of my projects follows on from a recent study I did into Neural Machine Translation where I trained a simple sequence-to-sequence model in Tensorflow to translate text from French to English. I would like to expand on this work and study this area a lot more.

The second project is just a fun game idea that I have, called One Word Story. The user and a bot collaborate to create a story, taking turns to say one word at a time. I hope to:

    - Develop a convincing Machine Learning solution to the generation of feasible stories
    - Create a Flask Web application
    - Incorporate speech recognition and text-to-speech into the app
